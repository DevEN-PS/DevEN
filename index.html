§05 — Workflow

From drag-and-drop to geographic sync

The same four stages, whether it's a 40-bus classroom feeder or a 12,000-bus transmission network.

01
Drag & Drop

Build your network on a fluid SLD canvas. Drop buses, generators, loads, and lines with deep aesthetic customization.

02
Map Sync

Plot substations on the live interactive map. Shift-click any bus or line to instantly link geographic coordinates to SLD properties.

03
Solve & Visualize

Run Load Flow or Short Circuit studies. Watch power flow directions and magnitudes overlay on the real-world map in real-time.

04
Save as .py

Projects save as streamlined .py files. All UI tweaks and geographic settings are perfectly preserved for your next session.
