§05 — Workflow

From one-line to sign-off

The same four stages, whether it's a 40-bus feeder or a 12,000-bus transmission network.

01
Import or draw

Bring in .raw, .etp, or .mdb — or start from a blank one-line on an infinite canvas.

02
Configure

Select IEC or ANSI, set the contingency list, and DevEN validates the model before solving.

03
Solve

Local or cloud solver — a 5,000-bus load flow returns in under two seconds.

04
Report & sign off

Standards-referenced report, exported and version-locked for the regulator or client.
